FAQ

What is Usakag Fashions?

Usakag fashions is a private registered company dealing in variety of products including Bags, men wears, women wear, scarves and veils.
We also do some IT works involving website development and designing, computer hardware repair and mentainance, networking and others.

What are servises delivered by Usakag Fashions?

We do door to door deliveries of products directly to our customers.
We also offer customer care services involving; directing customers to our sale points, giving discount to customers who come directly to our shops.

How do you access Usakag products?

You may contact us through our website @Usakag Fashions, or call our managing director Mr.Kagolo Usama on 0704544125,or come directly to Usafi market stall No.D029-D031, or come to our shops at William street @lockup besides Trade link No.504.

How can you become Usakag Fashions sales agent ?

You can contact our managing director Mr.Kagolo Usama on 0704544125, director human resource Male Huzairu on 0706926097.
